Items

Smash Ball Gives user a final smash. A final smash is quite smashing if you ask me
Assist Trophy Summons a random character to assist in battle
Poké-Ball Throwing it summons a random pokemon to assist in battle
Master-Ball Throwing it summons a random legendary pokemon to assist in battle. Possibility for a non-legendary pokemon
dragoon parts collecting all three parts gives user ability for a one hit KO move
Day Break Collecting all three parts creates a super weapon that shoots a powerful laser!
Bomber Curious little guy that wanders around on stage. Once someone picks him up and holds him up long enough...RUN
Blast Box A box that explodes with after a small period of time after getting hit. If hit by fire, it will blow up imediately
Fake Smash Ball A ball that looks similar to the real smash ball. If you break it, you're in for a painful surprise.
Sandbag A literal punching bag waiting to get walloped. Hitting it will cause items to fall out such as food or weapons.
Food Delicious treats lying around on the battle feild. Eat some to recover some health.
Maximum Tomato A delicious tomato from planet Popstar. Eat one to recover most of your health.
Heart Container A legendary relic from the gods of hyrule. Grab one to recover all of your health.
Fairy Bottle A classic fairy-trapped-in-a-bottle from hyrule. Open the bottle after reaching 100% in damage to recover 100% back.
Healing Sprout A magical orb that feels very nice. Hold it to gain as much health back as possible!
Healing Feild A plant that sprouts a healing feild. Stand in the aura to gain health back.
Super Mushroom The iconic mushroom with it's trademark ability to grow characters larger. It also makes them stronger as a result, but keep in mind it has a time limit.
Poison Mushroom Imagine the super mushroom but has the opposite effects. It will shrink you down, making you easier to launch. It looks similar to the poison mushroom, so proceed with caution.
Super Star The iconic star from the mario bros series. Grab it to become invincible for a short period of time.
Warp Star The legendary warp star that belongs to Kirby. Hop on it to fly up in the air and smash down onto the stage and KO any opponent you hit. Be careful not fly off the stage!
Metal Box A power-up that will temporarly turn you metal. While metal, you'll have increased power and be hard to launch.
Bunny Hood A head piece typically worn by the hero of time to increase his running speed. In smash bros, it can also increase jumping height!
Superspicy Curry A plate of curry that is so spicy it makes you breathe fire for a period of time. Be sure to get a nice cold glass of water afterwards.
Timer A mysterious pocket watch that can slow down time along with slowing down other fighters. Be aware that this item can backfire and slow you down instead.
Lightning Bolt The electrifying lightning bolt from the Mario Kart series. This item will shrink everyone down for a bit of time. This item has a possibility of backfiring.
Bullet Bill Using bullet bill will turn you into a bullet bill and dash in the direction you point towards and knock people out of the way
Beam Sword A laser sword made to deliver a lot of hurt. Use your side smash to swing the sword. Throwing the sword is also recommended.
Home-Run Bat The ultimate bat that will bring those hit by it a bad day. Getting hit by this bat results in an imediate KO.
Hammer The original hammer power-up from the old Donkey Kong arcades. While holding this temporary power up, walk into foes and send them flying.
Golden Hammer A powered up version of the hammer. Walk into opponents to give them a bad time.
Lip's Stick A daisy that can cause flower damage. Flower damage is small amounts of damage given throughout a period of time.
Fire Bar A classic obstacle turned into a weapon. Swing at opponents to smck them with fiery goodness!
Ore Club Swing this club to summon tornadoes. The longer you charge, the larger the tornado.
Killing Edge This sword will start and stop glowing red on a timer. If you hit someone with this sword while it's glowing red, you will deal a critical blow.
Death's Scythe The weapon of the grim reaper himself. If you hit someone with this while their damage percentage is over 100%, it will result in an imediate KO.
Ray Gun This ray gun's light beam has a reversed image of a smash ball printed on it. This let's the one on the wrong side of the gun "see smash balls" instead of "see stars"
Fire Flower The power up everyone knows and loves. Hold this up to someone to really "lighten up their day"
Super Scope A blaster gun designed after the original super scope from the NES. Press and hold the trigger button to fire a charge shot.
Gust Bellows A bellow used to blow people off the battlefeild. Keeping blowing air on them to prevent them from landing on the stage if you really want to get people angry!
Steel Diver A literal submarine turned into a gun. Shoots mini missles that start slow but really gets going.
Drill A two step weapon that lets you fire the drill out first, and then throw it's base. Both are strong in their own right.
Banana This banana gun shoots out a banana that is suprisingly extremely powerful. After you shoot the banana, you are left with the peel and a punchline to a joke.
Rage Blaster The higher your damage percentage is, the stronger this weapon will fire.
Ramblin' Evil Mushroom Another curious little guy that wanders around on stage. Pick it up and spray it's fumes on opponents to invert their controls.
Staff A staff made by the gods. It shoots out thin light beams. The farther the opponent, the stronger the attack.
Bob-omb The classic enemy with a real exposive personality. Thow at opponents to blast them away. However, if it's alone for a while, it get's bored and walks around.
Motion Sensor Bomb You can place this sneaky bomb on the floor to surprise foes with a blast. Note that you can get blown up by it too.
Green Shell Throw this to run along the floor and hit foes with comedic affect. Bouncing on it will stop it in it's tracks.
Bumper A real bump in the road to the feeling of being inside a pinball machine. Throw it somewhere and it will float in mid-air unmoving for some time.
Freezie A little guy that really brings the "freeze" in freezie. Throw it at someone to freeze them in their tracks.
Mr. Saturn This little shy fellow came from saturn valley. Throw him at someone with a sheild up to break their sheild.
Gooey Bomb When you throw this bomb at someone, be assured that it will "stick" to the plan. Even if the carrier sticks it right back on you.
Smart Bomb Despite the name, using this item isn't as complicated as you'd guess. Just a simple throw and watch 'em burn.
Smoke Ball Throw this ball to have it spit out rainbow colored smoke. It can also stick to people.
Pitfall Throw this at the ground to place a trap. Once someone steps on it, they get buried half way in the ground for a good ol' strong smash attack. Or endless taunts. Your choice.
Hothead When you throw this item, it will move along a surface and wrap around the abject it landed on. Hitting it before hand will cause it to deal more damage.
Banana Peel Throw this on the floor and wait for someone to slip on it. When that happens, you are legally obligated to laugh.
Unira A sea urchin on land makes for a bad day. Careful not to touch it's spikes.
Soccer Ball A soccer ball that sits and rolls around on stage. When hit, the ball will light on fire and get flung off the direction it was hit.
Bombchu After thrown, it scurries around like a mouse, and can climb walls and ceilings. Once it comes in contact...BOOM!
X bomb When the bomb is thrown, fire blasts will blow up in an 'x' shape. This shape may be shaped as a '+' too.
Hocotate Bomb The Hocotate ship basically turned into a bomb. When you set it on the floor, it will take off...only to crash a few seconds later.
POW Block You can throw the POW block onto the ground 3 times in a row before it's all used up. When it is hit, the floor will shake violently and all grounded opponents take damage.
Spiny Shell This shell has terrorized mario kart players for years. It will home in on whoever's in first place and land on them causing a big blue explosion.
Boomerang A boomerang that functions exctly how you'd expect it too. Be careful, the opponent can grab it too.
Beetle Although it looks pathetic, stay out of this little demon's way. If it grabs you, you take off to parts unknown.
Cucco What looks like a normal rooster is really a pain in the neck, depending on who it decides to attack. Hitting it will make a flock of cucco attack you.
Beehive Throw this at someone to get a horde of bees angry at them and begin stinging. Not only is it painful, it's also annoying to the one affected.
Killer Eye this item acts as a turret, sitting in one place and firing automatically.
Boss Galaga Coming all the back from Galaga from the arcades, this item is here to abduct anyone you're fighting! Simply throw it and it will do the rest of the work.
Beastball A baseball that has mysterious side affects. Throw it anywhere and it will disappear and teleport to hit someone in surprise.
Black Hole When the black hole is thrown, it will pull any opponents caught in it's vortex towards the center, leaving them open for attacks.
Franklin Badge This badge has a deep importance in the Mother series. When equipped, it will reflect any projectiles that touches you.
Screw Attack This is typically an upgrade for Samus's suit, but in smash anything goes. Wear it to have access to an electric summersault jump attack.
Back Sheild This sheild is able to hover all it's own. It will always cover your back...or at leeast until the timer runs out.
Super Leaf Equip this power up to gain the ability to hover in the air for a bit of time, allowing for you to recover better.
Rocket Belt Despite it's name, it's a jetpack that does what all jetpacks should do, let you fly high.
Super Launch Star This is a tool mario used to travel in the mario galaxy series. Here, you can throw it to set a launch star yourself and send people packing!
Special Flag This flag is as old as videogames itself. Hold it up in the air long enough to regain a stock.
Crates There are lots of items, so typically you'd want to be able to store them somewhere. Luckily, crates do just that! Break them open to get hidden goodies.
Rolling Crates Basically the same as crates, however these ones have wheels to roll on.
Barrel Another item similar to crates, however these will roll around a lot more. Knock them into someone to deal lots of damage.
Capsule These also are similar to crates, however it will only ever ontain one item. Sometimes, they blow up!
Party Ball The last of the "like crates" type of item. This one will float up in the air and spill out goodies or bombs.
Grass These locks of grass will occasionally sprout on the battle feild. Pull on them to reveal a hidden item!
