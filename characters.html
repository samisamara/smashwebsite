characters

01 mario

It's-a-me, Mario! This overall-wearing plumber finds himeslf saving princesses from giant turtles approximately once a week. Press B to throw his all famous fireballs!

02 donkey kong

King of kongs, wearer of ties, savior of bananas, Donkey Kong is here to smash! If he really wants to, he can slam his fists on the floor over and over to cause earthquakes!

03 link

The hero who hails from the hyrule kingdom is ready for action! He has plenty of tricks up his sleeve, like arrows, boomerangs, and a remote controlled bomb!

04 samus

Samus is locked, loaded, and ready for action! Samus travels through outer space to search for her next bounty, so she is equiped with a variety of tools such as missles and even a plasma blaster!

04ε dark samus

There once was a metroid prime that absorbed Samus's Phazon Suit and her DNA. Dark Samus is what resulted from the incident. While Samus's suit is nothing more than armor, Dark Samus's suit IS it's body.
Any character with a "ε" symbol means they are an echo fighter. An echo fighter is a fighter based on another character's moveset.

05 yoshi

Yoshi is a dinasoar who once helped mario get back home when he was just a baby. He also has the ability to lay eggs and throw them at his enemies!

06 kirby

He's lean, he's mean, he's a pink puffy machine! Kirby is here all the way from dreamland for some cake! Kirby has the ability to inhale is opponents and copy their abilities! Press B to inhale an opponent!

07 fox

"Do a barrel roll!" This quote came from fox's teamate, Peppy. Fox is quick on his feet, and has a laser gun and a reflector.

08 pikachu

Pikachu is one cheeky little pokemon, sporting electricity and tail attacks to boot. His cheeks contain red poutches that generates all of his electricity.

09 luigi

Always known as the lesser of the two brothers, Luigi is scared of just about anything, including his own shadow. However, he really got the time to shine in his own series: Luigi's Mansion!

10 ness

This baseball cap wearing kid comes from a little town to use his PK attacks to really mix up his foes! Be sure to be extra gentle with the bumble bees.

11 captain facon

"Falcon Punch!" Who else is worthy of such a famous move? Not only is he powerful, he is also an expert racer!

12 jigglypuff

Jigglypuff, the "balloon pokemon" is able to inflate itself with air to hover similar to Kirby. It also is able to put anyone to sleep with it's sing ability.

13 peach

The princess in distress, the one who's always (and I do mean always) kidnapped by bowser, or "in another castle" if you will. Well, I guess she felt like shaking things up because here she is in smash! Press down b to pull a turnip from the ground, a move that originates from Super Mario Bros 2!

13ε daisy

Daisy is the princess of Sarasaland. She is very similar to peach in terms of attacks, however she's much more energetic and wild in personality. You haven't seen true terror until you've heard Daisy say "Hi I'm Daisy!"

14 Bowser

The Koopa King himself, Bowser has joined the fight! He can breathe fire, use his shell to attack, and for his final smash, he turns into Giga Bowser! Hobbies include: go karting, tennis, kidnapping peach, and golf.

15 ice climbers

Originating from the NES game "Ice Climber" from 1985, these two little coat-covered kids reached super smash bros. Popo and Nana both attack together, and work best as a team, so keep that in mind during the fight!

16 sheik

In a world without the hero of time, the kingdom of hyrule fell into the hands of the demon king Ganondorf. Appearing from the shadows is Sheik, a ninja who is trying to guide heroes on the right path while keeping her secret identity. I mean his. Oops!

17 zelda

Hylia, the goddess of hyrule, one day gave birth to a daughter. That daughter is Zelda, the princess of the hyrule kingdom in every generation. Throughout the timeline, there will always exist a Zelda, as well as a hero in green. This specific Zelda comes from "The Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds".

18 dr. mario

If you're ever feeling ill, it's most likely because of space viruses making you feel sick so they can take over the planet. That or you have a cold. But whenever that happens, you can go straight to Dr. Mario! [Caution: is not a qualified doctor]

19 pichu

Pichu, the electric mouse pokemon, is the pre-evolution of Pikachu and Raichu. Because it is still a baby, it tends to shock itself when using it's electric moves.

20 falco

Falco is the wingman of fox in the "star fox". He has a bad mouth, and a bad attitude. He also turned his reflector into a weapon!

21 marth

Marth is a prince and comes from the videogame series "Fire Emblem". Remember that name because it will show up a lot. And I do mean A LOT. His sword is most affective when you land a hit with the tip of his sword, so try to dance around your opponents with this knowledge.

21ε lucina

Lucina comes from the future and is a descendant of Marth. You may be wondering: "Why is she in the present and why is she fighting her ancestor?" The answer is don't think about it.

22 young link

This specific Link is the hero of time. Because of his confrontation with Ganondorf, the timeline was split into 3 seperate timelines. Later on, he ventured to the land of Termina in the game "The Legend of Zelda Majora's Mask"

23 ganondorf

The demon king, the king of darkness, he goes by many names, but the original version is ganondorf, the male gerudo who is directly responsible for the pain of millions of lives in the hyrule kingdom spanning across thousands of years. Watch out for his sword and war-lock punch.

24 Mewtwo

Mewtwo was created in a lab by Blane, a scientist who previously worked for team rocket. Mewtwo is extremely powerful, as well as extremely dangerous. Keep watch for his shadow ball and disable attacks!

25 roy

Roy, just like Marth is a prince from Fire Emblem. He functions practically identical to Marth, however, his moves have a firey spin on things!

25ε chrom

Chrom is Lucina's father from...Fire Emblem. He is the echo fighter of Roy, except without the firey spin. So he's basically Marth but a bit different...but most of his moves come from roy? Fire Emblem has a lot of representation huh?

26 mr. game & watch

Mr. Game & Watch is from the "Game and Watch" series that existed back in the 1980s. They were very simple in code, and was the beginning of portable gaming. In battle, he's completely flat! He is a 2D character, just like his games. You better watch out for his judge attack, because when the hammer hits 9, you lose!

27 metaknight

Metaknight is the dark rival of Kirby. He weilds the galaxia sword, dawns the dimensional cape which allows him to teleport, and he took down Galacta Knight, the strongest warrior in the galaxy. Long story short, Meta Knight is one tough cookie.

28 pit

Pit is the server of the goddess Palutena, who grants him the power of flight. His weapon doubles as a dual sword and a bow that can shoot light arrows!

28ε dark pit

Dark pit was created with the mirror of truth, a mirror that is able to duplicate people and objects to be the exact opposite. While Pit is upbeat and faithful to Palutena, Dark Pit is much more moody and independant.

29 zero suit samus

Samus typically only fights in her phazon suit. But in this case, the gloves come off! She has a neat laser gun that doubles as an electric whip, and she's got some fancy rocket boots! I wonder how high she can fly with those things on...

30 wario

The bitter rival of Mario, or the "bad Mario" is here to fight, and he's packing garlic! Not only is he a mean motorcyclist, but he also has the power of...farting? Well in any case, you do NOT want to get caught in that fart cloud.

31 snake

To say Snake lived a simple life would be absolutely ridiculous. He has been constantly dragged into involvement with war, militaries, terrorist organizations, you name it. While his past may be troubled, he is a master weapons expert in combat. Rockets, bombs, hand-to-hand combat, he knows it all!

32 ike

Oh look, it's another Fire Emblem sword fighter...ain't that a surprise? He's your standard sword user with a lot of power...and that's all I got...

33-35 pokémon trainer

Squirtle! Ivysaur! Charizard! Go! This fighter is a three in one combo, replicating the use of multiple pokemon in a pokemon battle! Make sure to utilize each individual pokemon!

36 diddy kong

Diddy kong has been at Donkey Kong's side all the way back since Donkey Kong Country! While Donkey Kong is all muscle, Diddy on the other hand is wild and brings out his inner monkey! Or his outer monkey. Your take.

37 lucas

Lucas is a kid that comes from the Nowhere Islands. He is may be cowardly and shy, but can you blame him for the tragedy of his past? When we consider him in battle however, he borrows lots of tools and PK attacks from his friends. For instance, his rope snake comes from Duster!

38 sonic

Here comes sonic running around at the speed of sound! This hedgehog with an attitude is able to run at sonic speeds. Ever since the megadrive, he's been stopping the nefarious Dr. Eggman in his quest to take over the world and build his Eggman Empire! On his adventures, he made friends with plenty of animal buddies, like Tails, Knuckles, Amy Rose, Cream and Cheese, and more! Hold down and mash B to do a spindash!

39 king dedede

King Dedede is the bitter rival of Kirby...or at least he used to be. In Kirby Super Star Ultra, they settled their rivalry once and for all in the ring, and Kirby came victorious. Although the score was settled, King Dedede still holds a grudge. In battle, he's heavy penguin with a heavy mallet that can turn into a jet hammer!

40 olimar

Olimar comes from a distant planet. One day on his space ventures, he crash landed on on a distant planet. There he met the pikmin, and he needed to get their help in order to get home! In battle, olimar can use pikmin to deal plenty of damage to his opponents! He's a great leader, and that is applied to his moveset in smash!

41 lucario

Lucario the "aura pokemon" is a fighting-steel type pokemon. It has the ability to use aura to sense things around it. It also has the ability to mega evolve, a technique some pokemon can use to temporarly change their form.

42 rob

Rob was a toy that was packaged with every NES that was sold. In smash however, he's a robot that's packing lasers. Keep an eye on how brightly the light on his head is lit. The brighter the light, the stronger the laser.

43 toon link

Many, many years after the hero of time defeated Ganon, the demon king came back and reined terror upon hyrule. Soon, the world was flooded. Later on in the timeline, the hero of winds appeared, and re-sealed Ganondorf! Throughout his adventure, he traveled the great sea on a sentient boat named "The king of red lions".

44 wolf

Wolf acts as Fox's greatest adversary. His fight style greatly matches Fox. His laser gun on the other hand is slower than fox's, but is more powerful too.

45 villager

This curious little bug catcher somehow wound up becoming the mayor of a town, and even weirder is he got into smash! Although he looks cutesy, be forewarned of his pocket ability. This allows Villager to grab any projectile and stuff it in his pocket for later use!

46 megaman

That's right, the blue bomber himself is here to fight, and he's equiped with copied abilities to boot! Megaman was once just a lab assistant robot named Rock who was helping his creator: Dr. Light. When Dr. Wily began his schemes to take over the world, Rock volunteered to fight for justice, and dawned the name Megaman!

47 wii fit trainer

"Feel the burn" said the most enthusiastic mannequin in existance. Wii fit Trainer is a simple workout trainer, but looks like she felt like testing her abilities today! All of Wii Fit Trainer's moves are based off yoga poses and sports.

48 rosalina & luma

Once when Bowser kidnapped Peach along with her entire castle and blasted mario into space, Mario ended up meeting Rosalina, the adoptive mother of the lumas. She resides in the cosmic observatory, a space ship that doubles as a home built to explore the universe. She always carries a magic wand with a star-shaped tip.

49 little mac

"Hit 'em baby!" Little mac may be a short man, but boy does he pack a punch! He's unstoppable in the ring! As for smash...goodluck.

50 greninja

Greninja, the ninja pokemon, can throw throwing stars made up of water. It toys with it's opponents using swift motions. It's scarf is actually it's tongue.

51 mii brawler

The brawler of many faces. Mii Brawler can be customized to fit the outfit the player wishes. Mii Brawler's B moves can also be customized to different options.

52 mii swordfighter

The swordfighter of many faces. Mii Swordfighter can be customized to fit the outfit the player wishes. Mii Swordfighter's B moves can also be customized to different options.

53 mii gunner

The Gunner of many faces. Mii gunner can be customized to fit the outfit the player wishes. Mii Gunner's B moves can also be customized to different options.

54 palutena

Palutena is the goddess of light and ruler of Angel land. In smash, she can create light beams and attack with her staff. Her final smash creates a black hole and a mega laser.

55 pac-man

Yep, we're kicking it back old school! Pac-Man comes all the way back from 1980 and is one of the first videogame characters ever made! Perform a smash attack to summon one of his iconic ghosts!

56 robin

It's another Fire Emblem character...but hey, at least this one isn't just a swordfighter! Robin can perform magic spells such as fireballs and electric beams!

57 shulk

Shulk isn't just called a monado boy for nothing, he carries a blade called the monado. Shulk also has the ability to see the future, which allows him to counter surprise attacks.

58 bowser jr.

Bowser Jr is the son of the koopa king Bowser, and heir to the throne. He flies in the "koopa clown car" which is a veichle with a clown face capable of fight. It can even shoot cannon balls out of it's mouth!

59 duck hunt

This cheeky little dog has been laughing at dunt hunters for decades now, and boy do we all want to smack the grin off his face. In smash, he is partnered up with a duck who comes from the same game as him, and a sniper from beyond the screen.

60 ryu

Ryu comes from the world famous series: Street fighter! Ryu is a master of martial arts. Through his training, he learned to use the hadouken. Press up-B to have him use and say his famous phrase "SHORYUKEN!"

60ε ken

Ken is an echo fighter in super smash bros, but not only that, but he is one of the very first echo fighters in video game history! He has all of Ryu's fighting styles and moves, but with a fiery twist!

61 cloud

Cloud comes from the Final Fantasy series, more specifically Final Fantasy 7. Besides having a stupid haircut, he also weilds the buster sword!

62 corrin

Yeah I know it's yet ANOTHER Fire Emblem sword fighter. He's part dragon whoop-dee-doo.

63 bayonetta

Bayonetta is the umbra witch coming from a series named after her. She's EXTRA stylish, carrying lots of fancy guns. She even wears them on her shoes!

64 inkling

Inklings are creatures that live far in the future after the extinction of humans. They developed into a society similar to humans with their own fashion, cuture, sports, music, language, and more! They have the ability to shoot ink with their weapons and swim in their own ink!

65 ridley

Ridley is a space pirate that roams the universe, destroying whatever planet he can get his hands on. Samus has been hunting him down for years now, but he always comes back. Fun fact: Ridley killed Samus's parents...wait that's not fun at all!

66 simon

Hailing from the Castlevania series, Simon Belmont is here to fight! He has all of his classic weapons like his holy water and cross boomerang, but nothing beats his vampire killer whip! His up B isn't too great, so use the whip whenever you can to recover!

66ε richter

Richter comes from the Castlevania series just like Simon, however he comes from a different era. In fact, Simon Belmont is actually Richter's ancestor! Considering the fact that they will be fighting each other in smash makes for an awkward family renuion, though...

67 king k. rool

After long lasting anticaption, King K Rool is finally here to smash! He is the ultimate rival of Donkey Kong. Why? He stole Donkey Kong's banana horde of course! Not only is King K Rool packing a lot of muscle, but he also has his bluster gun, boomerang crown, and his signature belly armor!

68 isabelle

Isabelle is probably the most peaceful character ever, which makes her invitation to smash even more surprising! She has a pocket ability just like Villager, but also has a fishing hook to grab people from afar!

69 incineroar

Incineroar, the heel pokemon, is the final evolution of Litten. It has a love for wrestling and resembles a pro wrestler. The more hits it takes, the stronger he becomes.

70 pirahna plant

Pirahna Plant is an enemy that his been in the Mario series ever since the very beginning, surprising people by coming out of pipes. So of course he'd be in smash! Why are you surprised? Pirahna Plant can spit out spike balls and poisonous gas as well as stretch out to take chomps at opponents!

71 joker

Coming from Persona 5, Joker is here to steal your heart! Not only does Joker have his signature theif skills, but he also is equiped with Arsen, a representation of his soul!

72 hero

In the world of Dragon Quest, there exists many heroes of many eras in time similar to Link in the Zelda universe. Here in smash, you can play as 4 different heroes! Press down B to have a selection of random RPG moves!

73 banjo & kazooie

Oh yeah it's happening, Banjo & Kazooie are FINALLY back! They got all their class abilities too! They can triple jump, shoot egg gernades, use the feather dash, and more! It must be great to be back guh-huh?

74 terry

Terry is the iconic Mr. Tough guy from the Fatal Fury video game series. This series has a long history spanning back to the good ol' arcade era, and came out the same year as Street Fighter!
